Mahindra University, India
RoboVerse By Mahindra University

Roboverse is Mahindra University’s first ever Robotics Club that aims to encourage students to unleash their creativity and engineering skills through Robotics.
With informational workshops, compelling events, and hands on projects, Roboverse will indulge students in the rapidly expanding world of Robotics. Roboverse caters individuals with any level of experience & expertise.Join us & become the next Robo-geek!

Events 2022

Formula Air 2022

Mahindra University will host one of the top events, Formular Air. At Formula Air, racers will compete on a MultiGP track against pro pilots from across India, competing in three lap courses followed by workshops from pilots. A one-day event where professional pilots from all over the country visit MU and indulge in competitive drone racing. Event consists of a price pool upto 50k INR
World Robotics Olympiad, India

The World Robot Olympiad (WRO) is a global robotics competition for young people. The World Robot Olympiad competition uses Lego Mindstorms manufactured by LEGO Education which attracts more than 26,000 teams from more than 65 countries. WRO includes Regular, Open , WRO Football and ARC Catetory. Mahindra University will host WRO INDIA 2022 in Hyderabad which is a National event and bringing students all acorss India.
Screw It! - Lets Build Cool Stuff

As part of the vision of the club, the Roboverse club will host the series called Screw it! - Let's build cool sh!t which is a week-long event where club members will actively build and prototype robotic mechanisms and play around with sensors and combine multiple ideas to build robots.
